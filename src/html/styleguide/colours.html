Colour palette

The colour palette was designed with simplicity in mind

The idea of the colour system is the following:

  1. The first 3 (primary-tertiary) colours represent the brand
  2. The next 4 (quaternary - septenary) colours are for widely used 'base' colours (white, greys and black). These are used to represent the most basic typographical hierarchy in text and background;
  3. The last 3 colours (octonary - denary) are allocated for input states and feedback - mostly used in forms.
